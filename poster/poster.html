Background & Motivation
  • 5G multi-cell networks: each BS maximizes own throughput → inter-cell interference hurts fairness.
  • Goal: maximize Proportional-Fair utility U = Σu log(R̄u + ε) across all 12 UEs.
  • O-RAN constraint: each gNB observes only local UE metrics at runtime — no direct neighbor CSI exchange.
  • Insight: RIC xApp can compute a latent context z from KPM measurements and push it to gNBs via E2 interface — enabling coordination without violating the deployment line.

Analysis & Discussion
Why z works (geo_z)
  • BC warm-start unlocks z: pure RL gives drop_shuffle≈0; BC+1000 steps yields +2.278
  • KPM includes inter-BS distances → z encodes spatial interference topology
  • drop_shuffle > drop_zero → z content used, not just as offset
  • Longer training (400k vs 200k) improves z utilization: +1.43 → +2.28
Why ablations fail
  • +RSRP: actor already sees neighbor channel gains → z becomes redundant (drop_shuffle +1.43 → +0.26)
  • +Critic BC: encoder learns to satisfy pre-trained Q, not actor → z actively misleads policy (drop_shuffle −0.45)
HASAC Theory Validation
  • Sequential Soft Policy Decomposition (Liu et al., ICLR 2024 §3.3): each BS updates in random permutation → QRE (global MaxEnt optimum) vs sub-optimal NE
  • Empirical: sequential HASAC (no z) −2.581 vs simultaneous −5.184 (+2.6 PF-U improvement)
  • Open problem: SAC Q-overestimation causes oscillation after step ∼40–80k; mitigated by μ-bound+logpf reward
Conclusion
✅
C-HASAC > HASAC by +1.42 PF-U (−1.162 vs −2.581, 400k steps). The only difference: actor receives learned latent context z.
✅
z is genuinely used: drop_shuffle = +2.278. Replacing z with a mismatched episode's z hurts more than zeroing it — content matters, not just magnitude.
✅
BC warm-start is the catalyst: without BC, drop_shuffle ≈ 0 (z ignored). 1000 steps of expert imitation opens the z-usage switch.
✅
O-RAN deployable: z flows from RIC xApp → E2 interface → gNBs. No direct inter-BS CSI exchange; only locally observable KPMs used at runtime.
⚠
Large gap to PF-WSR ceiling (+23.529) remains; future work: conservative Q (CQL) to reduce overestimation and stabilize late training.
